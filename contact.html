Frequently Asked Questions

❓

Never played dodgeball before?

No worries! We welcome players of all skill levels. Our friendly team will teach you everything you need to know, from basic rules to advanced strategies.

👕

What should I bring?

Just bring yourself, comfortable sports clothes, and indoor sports shoes. We provide all the dodgeballs and equipment you need!

💰

How much does it cost?

Check out our Practice & Prices page for detailed membership options. We offer flexible plans to fit every budget and commitment level!

🎯

Can I try before joining?

Absolutely! Come to any training session for a free trial. Experience the DCA energy firsthand and see if dodgeball is your new passion!
