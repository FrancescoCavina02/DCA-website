Hear from the Founders

Three visionaries who threw the first ball and started it all. Here's their story of how DCA came to life.

💡

The Spark

Founder Name #1

The nearest practice was in Haarlem, while we lived in Amsterdam. This sparked the idea: a clear opportunity to build a dodgeball community right here, closer to home.

🏗️

Building the Foundation

Founder Name #2

Our goal was high-level training nearby, but new players weren't instantly ready. We built from existing communities, secured a location, and trained people to raise our level.

🌟

The Vision

Founder Name #3

We dreamed of creating more than just a sports club - a community where everyone belongs, regardless of skill level. Today, seeing DCA members forming lifelong friendships and representing the Netherlands internationally... that's the dream realized!
