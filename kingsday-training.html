👑 Kingsday Training

Royal dodgeball sessions in dazzling orange

🧡 Royal Training Tradition

Every year on Kingsday, Amsterdam transforms into a sea of orange, and so do we! Our special Kingsday training sessions are legendary – where national pride meets dodgeball passion in the most spectacular way possible.

Picture this: the entire team decked out in vibrant orange, from head to toe, creating a stunning visual spectacle as we train with extra enthusiasm. It's not just about the sport; it's about celebrating Dutch culture while perfecting our dodgeball skills. The energy is infectious, the atmosphere electric, and the team spirit absolutely royal!
