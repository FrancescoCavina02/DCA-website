Discover DCA!

Learn about who we are

Dodgeball Club Amsterdam (DCA) brings together players of all levels for high-energy dodgeball. We're part of the Dutch League of Dodgeball (DBN) and meet twice a week to train, compete, and have a blast.

We're proud to be part of Student Sport Amsterdam (SSA), the foundation supporting student sports across Amsterdam. SSA connects student athletes with sports clubs, organizes events, and works with university sports centers to make sports more accessible. As an SSA-affiliated club, we can offer students an exclusive 11% discount on 1-year memberships‚Äîmaking it easier than ever to join our dodgeball community!
