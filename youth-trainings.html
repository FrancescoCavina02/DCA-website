Got Questions?

Here are answers to the most common questions from parents and young athletes

🎯

What is a pilot training?

A pilot training is a practice run that helps us test and refine the program. We'll gather feedback from players and parents to make adjustments before the regular sessions begin.

👕

What should my child bring?

Just comfortable sports clothes, indoor sports shoes, and a water bottle. All dodgeball equipment is provided by DCA!

🆕

Never played dodgeball before?

Perfect! Our program is designed for all skill levels. Our coaches will teach everything from the basics to advanced strategies.

👥

Can parents watch?

Absolutely! Parents are welcome to observe training sessions and see their young athletes develop their dodgeball skills.
