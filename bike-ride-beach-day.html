Bike Ride & Beach Day

An Epic Adventure Through Dunes & Waves

Our Beach Adventure

We started at Haarlem station and went by bike to the beach. We took a beautiful bike ride through the dunes where we saw some wildlife🐴.


When we arrived at the beach we picknicked and after that it was time to chill, relax, and play some games. Some of us enjoyed the sun ☀️ others enjoyed the water🏊🏻‍♂️🏊🏻‍♀️.


On the way back we had dinner at an Italian restaurant in Haarlem. We had a great time and it was a great way to end the day! 🍕🍝
