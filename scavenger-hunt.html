DCA Scavenger Hunt – What. A. Day.

It's been a week since we turned the streets of Amsterdam into our dodgeball court. For those who sadly missed it, here's a little recap:

What. A. Day.

From stickers everywhere, to romantic (???) dodgeball poses and pick-up lines, penguin and baby elephant mascots, questionable paprika-dodgeballs, a not-so-successful attempt at youth recruitment (yes, someone might have made a child cry)… and many more highlights - the day was packed with chaos in the best way.

Again congrats to the winning team - led with almost German-like efficiency and time management skills (nope, not talking about myself). Meanwhile, the other team fully embraced the snacking with ice-cream and hot dog stops along the way 😬

Thanks again to everyone who showed up and brought the DCA spirit. Can't wait to see many of you at the next social event! ♥️
